About MD Writers

Based in Lagos with a reach across the globe, MD Writers is a premium ghostwriting and publishing agency dedicated to turning bold ideas into standout books, brands, and content. Our team combines deep creative expertise with sharp publishing know-how to deliver work that is original, confidential, and always on schedule.

We handle everything from full-length ghostwriting across memoirs, business books, cookbooks, and travel guides, to expert editing, KDP optimization, cover design, personal branding, and hands-on publishing training. What truly defines us is our focus on your unique voice. We listen closely, collaborate openly, and produce content that resonates deeply with readers while elevating your brand or personal legacy.

Authors, entrepreneurs, and creators choose us because we deliver results that matter: higher sales, stronger reviews, and lasting impact. Ready to bring your story to life? Let's make it exceptional.
